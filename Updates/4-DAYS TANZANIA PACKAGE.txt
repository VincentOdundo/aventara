4-DAYS TANZANIA PACKAGE  
Day 1: Arusha to Serengeti National Park
After breakfast in Arusha, you will depart for Serengeti National Park, enjoying a scenic drive through the beautiful landscapes of northern Tanzania. En route, you will pass through the Ngoro Ngoro Conservation Area, with opportunities to admire the breathtaking views and wildlife along the way. Upon arrival in the Serengeti, you will enjoy an afternoon game drive as you head toward your campsite. Dinner and overnight will be at the camp, surrounded by the sounds of the African wilderness.


Day 2: Full Day in Serengeti National Park
Today is dedicated to a full-day game drive in Serengeti National Park, one of Africa’s most renowned wildlife destinations. With picnic lunch boxes provided, you will explore the vast plains in search of the Big Five and other remarkable wildlife species. The Serengeti’s endless savannah offers excellent opportunities for photography and close observation of animals in their natural habitat. In the later in the afternoon, you will return to the campsite for dinner and overnight stay.
Day 3: Serengeti to Ngoro Ngoro Conservation Area
After breakfast at the camp in Serengeti National Park, you will enjoy a morning game drive before departing for the Ngoro Ngoro Conservation Area. The journey offers spectacular scenery and possible wildlife sightings along the way. You will arrive at the camping site on the crater rim in the late afternoon, where you can relax and take in the stunning views of the crater below. Dinner and overnight will be at the campsite.
Day 4: Ngoro Ngoro Crater to Arusha
After an early breakfast, you will descend into the Ngoro Ngoro Crater for a halfday game drive. The crater is home to a high concentration of wildlife, offering excellent chances to spot a variety of animals in a unique ecosystem. After a picnic lunch, you will ascend the crater and begin your return journey to Arusha, arriving in the evening with unforgettable safari memories.
