
Day 1: Nairobi – Maasai Mara 
Your safari begins with an early morning pickup from your hotel or Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi. You will drive through the scenic Great Rift Valley, stopping briefly at the viewpoint for photography before continuing to Maasai Mara. Arriving at your camp in time for lunch, you can relax and freshen up before heading out for your first evening game drive. As the sun sets over the savannah, spot lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and buffaloes, with the golden light creating perfect photo opportunities. Return to the camp for dinner and overnight stay, soaking in the sights and sounds of the Mara at dusk.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner 
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp
Day 2: Full-Day Game Drive in Maasai Mara
After an early breakfast, embark on a full-day game drive across the Maasai Mara. With your packed picnic lunch, explore different areas of the reserve in search of the Big Five: lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino, alongside cheetahs, hyenas, gazelles, and a wide variety of birds. Visit the Mara River if time permits, renowned for dramatic wildlife activity, including the seasonal Great Migration crossings. Pause for a scenic picnic lunch in the heart of the reserve before continuing with afternoon game viewing. Return to your camp in the late afternoon, where dinner and overnight await.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner 
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp
Day 3: Maasai Mara – Lake Nakuru 
After breakfast, check out and depart Maasai Mara for Lake Nakuru National Park. En route, if time allows, you may pass by Lake Naivasha for optional activities at an extra cost, including a boat ride on the lake or a guided walking safari at Crescent Island at an extra cost, where giraffes, zebras, and antelopes roam freely. Continue your journey to Lake Nakuru and arrive at your hotel in the afternoon. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure, relaxing and taking in the scenic views of the park before dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
* Accommodation: Buraha Zenoni Hotel & Resort
Day 4: Lake Nakuru –Amboseli
Begin the day with an early morning game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, famous for its rhino sanctuary and abundant wildlife. You may spot both black and white rhinos, Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, and flocks of flamingos (seasonal) along the lakeshore. After the morning game drive, depart Lake Nakuru and head towards Amboseli National Park, enjoying the scenic landscapes along the way. Arrive at your camp in Amboseli late in the evening, check in, have dinner, and relax for the night.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp
Day 5: Full-Day Game Drive in Amboseli
After an early breakfast, depart for a full day of game viewing in Amboseli National Park. The park is renowned for its large elephant herds set against the backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest peak. Spend the morning exploring the park to spot lions, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, hyenas, and a variety of birds. Have a picnic lunch in the park therefore continue with your game drive focusing on areas near swamps and waterholes where elephants, buffaloes, and other wildlife gather. Capture stunning sunset photos with Mount Kilimanjaro in the background before returning to your camp for dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp
Day 6: Amboseli – Nairobi
On your final day, after breakfast, check out of your camp and begin the scenic drive back to Nairobi. Along the way, enjoy the rolling savannah landscapes and rural villages of southern Kenya. Arrive in Nairobi in the afternoon and be dropped off at your hotel or Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, concluding your unforgettable six-day safari adventure.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch 
* No accommodation
