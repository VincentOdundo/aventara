Day 1: Arusha to Serengeti National Park
Your safari begins with a morning departure from Arusha, traveling through the picturesque landscapes of the Great Rift Valley and the Ngoro Ngoro Highlands. As you journey toward Serengeti National Park, you will enjoy scenic views of rolling plains and traditional Maasai villages. Upon entering the Serengeti, renowned for its endless golden savannahs and dramatic skies, you will experience some afternoon game drive rich with wildlife sightings. The park’s breathtaking beauty and vast open spaces create a truly unforgettable atmosphere. Dinner and overnight will be at Nyani Camp.

Day 2: Full Day in Serengeti National Park
Today is devoted to exploring the magnificent Serengeti, one of Africa’s most celebrated wildlife sanctuaries. Its sweeping plains, acacia-dotted horizons, and seasonal rivers support an extraordinary concentration of wildlife. You may witness prides of lions, cheetahs on the hunt, elephants roaming freely, and large herds of wildebeest and zebras. The serene beauty of sunrise and sunset across the savannah offers remarkable photographic moments. With a picnic lunch in the bush, you will enjoy uninterrupted exploration before returning to Nyani Camp for dinner and overnight.
Day 3: Serengeti to Ngorongoro Conservation Area
After breakfast, you will depart Serengeti with a scenic game drive en route to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. The journey gradually transitions from vast plains to lush highlands, offering spectacular panoramic views. Ngoro Ngoro’s landscape is characterized by rolling green hills and cool mountain air, creating a striking contrast to the Serengeti. Upon arrival near the crater rim, you will appreciate the dramatic scenery surrounding this natural wonder. Dinner and overnight will be at Simba Campsite, ideally located for the following day’s exploration.
Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Full-Day Exploration
Today you will descend into the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Africa’s most breathtaking natural treasures. The crater floor, enclosed by towering walls, forms a unique ecosystem teeming with wildlife, including lions, elephants, buffaloes, flamingos, and the rare black rhinoceros. Its lush grasslands, acacia forests, and shimmering soda lake create a landscape of remarkable beauty. A picnic lunch will be enjoyed beside a hippo pool before ascending in the afternoon and returning to Simba Campsite for dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Lake Manyara National Park 
Following breakfast, you will travel to Lake Manyara National Park, a scenic gem nestled at the base of the Great Rift Valley escarpment. The park is renowned for its lush groundwater forests, sparkling alkaline lake, and vibrant birdlife, including flocks of flamingos. You may encounter tree-climbing lions, elephants, giraffes, and playful baboons within this compact yet diverse ecosystem. After a rewarding game drive and picnic lunch, you will proceed to Migombani Campsite for dinner and a comfortable overnight stay.
Day 6: Return to Arusha
On your final day, you will enjoy breakfast at Migombani Campsite before beginning the scenic drive back to Arusha. The journey offers beautiful views of

the highlands, local farms, and rural communities, providing a glimpse into
Tanzania’s cultural landscape. Upon arrival in Arusha, you will be transferred to your hotel or may opt for a brief city tour, depending on your schedule. This concludes your memorable safari, filled with extraordinary wildlife encounters and the natural beauty of Tanzania’s iconic parks.
