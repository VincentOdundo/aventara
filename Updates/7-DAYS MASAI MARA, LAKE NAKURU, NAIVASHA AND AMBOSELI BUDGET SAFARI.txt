
DAY 1: NAIROBI – MASAI MARA 
Pick up from your hotel or airport in Nairobi and depart for Maasai Mara via the scenic Great Rift Valley with a stopover at the viewpoint.
Arrive in time for lunch at your camp and relax.
In the afternoon, enjoy an evening game drive in Maasai Mara. Spot lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and other wildlife as the sun sets across the savannah.
Return to your camp for dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp 

DAY 2: FULL-DAY GAME DRIVE IN MASAI MARA
After early breakfast, depart with picnic lunch boxes for a full-day game drive.
Explore different sections of the reserve in search of the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino), cheetahs, hyenas, antelopes, and numerous bird species. You may visit the Mara River, famous for the Great Migration crossings (seasonal).
Enjoy picnic lunch inside the park and continue with afternoon game viewing.
Return to your camp in the late afternoon.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan:Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp 


 DAY 3: MASAI MARA – LAKE NAKURU (OPTIONAL STOP AT LAKE NAIVASHA)
After breakfast, check out and depart for Lake Nakuru National Park.
En route, if time allows, you may pass by Lake Naivasha for optional activities at an extra cost such as:
* Boat ride safari on the lake (20$ Per Person)
* Guided walking safari at Crescent Island (33$ Per Person)
Proceed to Lake Nakuru arriving in the afternoon for check-in to your budget hotel and relaxation.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park 
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Buraha Zenoni Hotel & Resort


DAY 4: LAKE NAKURU-NAIVASHA
After early breakfast, enjoy a morning game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, a renowned rhino sanctuary home to both white and black rhinos. The park is also famous for Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, buffaloes, and flamingos (seasonal).
After the game drive, exit the park and proceed to Lake Naivasha.
Arrive in Naivasha in time for lunch at your hotel and relax for the rest of the afternoon at leisure.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park 
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Leisure Apex Resort

DAY 5: NAIVASHA – AMBOSELI NATIONAL PARK
After breakfast at your hotel in Naivasha you will visit Hell’s Gate then you will depart for Amboseli National Park. The drive takes approximately 6–7 hours, passing through scenic landscapes of the Great Rift Valley, local villages, and farmlands. Along the way, you will have the opportunity to enjoy spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro looming on the horizon. Upon arrival in Amboseli in the evening you will check into your camp, relax, and freshen up before dinner. Depending on time and energy, you may enjoy a short evening walk around the camp to spot nearby wildlife or simply unwind while enjoying the breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro at sunset. Dinner and overnight are at the camp, preparing you for a full day of wildlife adventures the next day.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp


 DAY 6: FULL-DAY GAME DRIVE IN AMBOSELI
After an early breakfast, you will embark on a morning game drive in Amboseli, which is famous for its large elephant herds and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro. The morning is the best time to spot wildlife as animals are most active during the cooler hours. During the game drive, you may encounter lions, cheetahs, hyenas, zebras, giraffes, buffaloes, and hundreds of bird species, including pelicans and storks. Around midday, you will have a picnic lunch in the park. Later in the afternoon, you will embark on another game drive, focusing on the swamps and water sources where elephants and buffaloes congregate. As the sun sets, capture incredible photographs of wildlife with Mount Kilimanjaro as a backdrop. Return to the camp in the evening for dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp


DAY 7: AMBOSELI – NAIROBI
On the final day, after breakfast at the camp, you have the option for a short early morning game drive to enjoy one last chance to see elephants and other wildlife in the cool morning hours. Afterward, you will begin the scenic drive back to Nairobi. You will arrive in Nairobi in the afternoon and be dropped off at your hotel or Airport, concluding your unforgettable seven-day safari adventure.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch 
* No Accommodation
