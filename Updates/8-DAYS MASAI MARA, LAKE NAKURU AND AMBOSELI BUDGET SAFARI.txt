
Day 1: Nairobi – Maasai Mara 
Your safari begins with an early morning pickup from your hotel or Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi. You will drive through the scenic Great Rift Valley, stopping briefly at viewpoints for photography before reaching Maasai Mara in time for lunch at your camp. After settling in, embark on your first evening game drive, where you can spot lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and buffaloes as the Mara comes alive at sunset. Return to the camp for dinner and overnight stay, absorbing the sounds and sights of the savannah.
Main destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp

Day 2: Full-Day Game Drive in Maasai Mara
After an early breakfast, set out for a full-day game drive across Maasai Mara. With a packed picnic lunch, explore multiple areas of the reserve in search of the Big Five: lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino, as well as cheetahs, hyenas, gazelles, and a variety of birds. Visit the Mara River if time allows, famous for dramatic wildlife activity, including the seasonal Great Migration crossings. Return to the camp in the late afternoon for dinner and overnight stay.
Main destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp


Day 3: Second Full-Day Game Drive in Maasai Mara
Spend a second full day in Maasai Mara to maximize wildlife viewing. After breakfast, depart for a morning game drive to explore new areas of the park, giving ample opportunity to observe predators, grazing herds, and abundant birdlife. Stop for a picnic lunch in a scenic location within the park before continuing with an afternoon drive. Witness the savannah at sunset and return to your lodge for dinner and overnight stay.
Main destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp

Day 4: Maasai Mara – Lake Nakuru (Optional Stop at Lake Naivasha)
After breakfast, check out and depart Maasai Mara for Lake Nakuru National Park. On the way, if time allows, you may make a brief stop at Lake Naivasha for optional activities at an extra cost, such as a short boat ride on the lake or a walking safari on Crescent Island, where giraffes, zebras, and antelopes roam freely. Continue your journey to Lake Nakuru and arrive at your hotel in the afternoon. After checking in and relaxing, enjoy dinner and overnight stay.
Main destination: Lake Nakuru National Park
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Buraha Zenoni Hotel & Resort
Day 5: Game Drive in Lake Nakuru National Park
After breakfast, embark on a morning and afternoon game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, renowned for its rhino sanctuary and vibrant birdlife, including seasonal flamingos. You will have the chance to see both black and white rhinos, Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, buffaloes, and numerous waterbirds along the lake’s edge. Midday, have a picnic lunch.Dinner and overnight are at the hotel.
Main destination: Amboseli National Park
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp
Day 6: Lake Nakuru – Amboseli National Park
After breakfast, check out and drive to Amboseli National Park, famous for its large elephant herds and breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro. The journey takes several hours, with scenic stops along the way for photography and refreshments. Arrive at your camp in the late afternoon, check in, relax, and enjoy dinner before overnight stay.
Main destination: Amboseli National Park
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp



Day 7: Full-Day Game Drive in Amboseli
After an early breakfast, depart for a full-day game drive in Amboseli National Park. The morning offers the best opportunity to see elephants, lions, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and hyenas, with Mount Kilimanjaro providing a spectacular backdrop. Have a picnic lunch in the park therefore continue with your game drive focusing on waterholes where wildlife gathers. Capture stunning sunset photos before returning to the camp for dinner and overnight stay.
Main destination: Amboseli National Park
Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner 
Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp
Day 8: Amboseli – Nairobi
On the final day, enjoy breakfast at the camp, with the option of a short early morning game drive to see wildlife one last time. Afterward, exit the park and drive back to Nairobi. Arrive in Nairobi in the afternoon and be dropped off at your hotel or Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, concluding your eight-day Kenyan safari adventure.
Main destination: Nairobi
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch 
No Accommodation
