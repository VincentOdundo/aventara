3 Days Serengeti & Ngorongoro Safari

This short safari offers an unforgettable journey through two of Tanzania’s most iconic destinations: Serengeti National Park and the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater within the Ngoro Ngoro Conservation Area. The experience combines spectacular landscapes, scenic
drives, and immersive game viewing in some of Africa’s most renowned natural environments.

Day 1: Arusha – Serengeti National Park
Your safari begins with an early morning departure from Arusha, heading toward the vast plains of Serengeti National Park. The journey passes through the scenic highlands of the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, offering beautiful landscapes and panoramic views along the way.

After lunch, continue into the Serengeti where you will enjoy an afternoon game drive across the famous plains of the park.
In the evening, proceed to your lodge or tented camp for dinner and overnight stay. 
Accommodation: Nyani Camp

Day 2: Serengeti – Ngorongoro Conservation Area
After breakfast, enjoy a morning game drive in Serengeti National Park, taking in the vast landscapes and open plains that make the park one of the most famous wildlife destinations in the world.

Later, begin your journey toward the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, enjoying scenic views during the drive.

You will arrive at the Ngorongoro Crater rim in the late afternoon where you can relax and enjoy the spectacular scenery.
Accommodation: Simba Campsite 

Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater – Arusha
After an early breakfast, descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a half-day game drive. The crater is one of Africa’s most remarkable natural attractions, known for its unique ecosystem and spectacular scenery.

After enjoying a picnic lunch inside the crater, ascend back to the rim and begin the return journey to Arusha.

Arrival in Arusha is expected in the late afternoon, marking the end of your safari.
