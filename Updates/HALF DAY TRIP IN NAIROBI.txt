NAIROBI HALF DAY TRIP 
Pick up from your hotel at 8AM in Nairobi and drive to Nairobi National Park, located just 7 km from the city center. Embark on a half-day game drive through this unique park, home to lions, leopards, rhinos, giraffes, buffaloes, jackals, and a variety of birds. Enjoy wildlife against the Nairobi skyline and take plenty of photos. 
Visit to Giraffe Centre
Depart Nairobi National Park at 10:30AM and head to the Giraffe Centre. Spend time feeding and interacting with endangered Rothschild giraffes, learning about conservation efforts and the importance of protecting these gentle giants.
David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage Feeding
Arrive at the David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage at 11:00 AM to witness the daily baby elephant feeding session. Learn about the rescue and rehabilitation of orphaned elephants and hear their inspiring stories before they are gradually reintroduced to the wild.
Return to Nairobi Hotel
After the elephant orphanage visit, enjoy hotel drop-off in Nairobi, concluding your half-day wildlife adventure.
