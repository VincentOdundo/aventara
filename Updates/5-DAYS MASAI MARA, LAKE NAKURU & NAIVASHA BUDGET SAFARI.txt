
DAY 1: NAIROBI ñ MASAI MARA 
Pick up from your hotel or airport in Nairobi and depart for Maasai Mara via the scenic Great Rift Valley with a stopover at the viewpoint.
Arrive in time for lunch at your camp and relax.
In the afternoon, enjoy an evening game drive in Maasai Mara. Spot lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and other wildlife as the sun sets across the savannah.
Return to your camp for dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp 

DAY 2: FULL-DAY GAME DRIVE IN MASAI MARA
After early breakfast, depart with picnic lunch boxes for a full-day game drive.
Explore different sections of the reserve in search of the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino), cheetahs, hyenas, antelopes, and numerous bird species. You may visit the Mara River, famous for the Great Migration crossings (seasonal).
Enjoy picnic lunch inside the park and continue with afternoon game viewing.
Return to your camp in the late afternoon.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve 
* Meal Plan:Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp 


 DAY 3: MASAI MARA ñ LAKE NAKURU (OPTIONAL STOP AT LAKE NAIVASHA)
After breakfast, check out and depart for Lake Nakuru National Park.
En route, if time allows, you may pass by Lake Naivasha for optional activities at an extra cost such as:
* Boat ride safari on the lake (20$ Per Person)
* Guided walking safari at Crescent Island (33$ Per Person)
Proceed to Lake Nakuru arriving in the afternoon for check-in to your budget hotel and relaxation.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park 
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Buraha Zenoni Hotel & Resort


DAY 4: LAKE NAKURU-NAIVASHA
After early breakfast, enjoy a morning game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, a renowned rhino sanctuary home to both white and black rhinos. The park is also famous for Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, buffaloes, and flamingos (seasonal).
After the game drive, exit the park and proceed to Lake Naivasha.
Arrive in Naivasha in time for lunch at your hotel and relax for the rest of the afternoon at leisure.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park 
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Leisure Apex Resort

DAY 5: HELLíS GATE NATIONAL PARK ñ RETURN TO NAIROBI
After breakfast, visit Hellís Gate National Park for a unique safari experience where you can:
* Enjoy cycling among wildlife
* Take a guided walking safari
* Explore scenic gorges and cliffs
* View zebras, giraffes, gazelles, buffaloes, and various bird species
After the excursion, depart for Nairobi arriving in the afternoon. Drop-off at your hotel or airport.
* Main Destination: Nairobi
* Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch 
* Accommodation
