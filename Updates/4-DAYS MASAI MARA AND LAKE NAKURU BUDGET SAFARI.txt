
DAY 1: NAIROBI – MAASAI MARA 
Your safari begins with an early morning pick-up from your hotel or airport in Nairobi. Drive through the scenic Great Rift Valley with a stop at the viewpoint for photos before proceeding to Maasai Mara.
Arrive in time for lunch at your lodge or camp and enjoy some relaxation.
In the afternoon, head out for an evening game drive in the reserve. This is an excellent time to spot lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, and other wildlife as they become active before sunset.
Return to your camp for dinner and overnight.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve
* Meal Plan: Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp

 DAY 2: FULL-DAY GAME DRIVE 
After early breakfast, depart with picnic lunch boxes for a full-day game drive across the vast plains of Maasai Mara.
Explore different areas of the reserve in search of the Big Five (lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino) as well as cheetahs, hyenas, wildebeests, antelopes, and numerous bird species. You may visit the Mara River, known for its dramatic wildlife activity and the Great Migration crossings (seasonal).
Enjoy your picnic lunch at a scenic spot within the park and continue with afternoon game viewing.
Return to the camp in the late afternoon for dinner and overnight.
* Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Miti Mingi Eco Camp

 DAY 3: MAASAI MARA – LAKE NAKURU NATIONAL PARK
After breakfast, check out and depart for Lake Nakuru National Park. Enjoy a scenic drive through the Great Rift Valley landscapes.
Arrive in Nakuru in time for lunch at your hotel.
In the afternoon, proceed for a game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, famous for its rhino sanctuary and large populations of white and black rhinos. The park is also home to flamingos (seasonal), Rothschild giraffes, lions, leopards, buffaloes, and various bird species.
Return to your Hotel for dinner and overnight.
* Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Buraha Zenoni Hotel

 DAY 4: LAKE NAKURU – NAIROBI
After breakfast, enjoy a morning game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park for a final opportunity to spot wildlife and capture beautiful views of the lake.
Later, exit the park and drive back to Nairobi, arriving in the afternoon. Drop-off at your hotel or airport.

Main Destination: Nairobi 
Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch 
No Accommodation.
