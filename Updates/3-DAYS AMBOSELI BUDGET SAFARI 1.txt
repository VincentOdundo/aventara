DAY I: TRANSFER TO AMBOSELI 
Pick up from the hotel or the airport at the agreed time to join a group to Amboseli National Park, arriving in the evening if time allows, you will have an evening game drive till sunset. Amboseli National Park is famous for its huge Jumbo Elephants, including several species of wild animals and birds. A wide range of animals and bird species are found in this beautiful ecosystem, too. Your dinner and overnight stay will be at the camp.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Dinner Only
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp 
DAY 2: AMBOSELI FULL DAY GAME DRIVE 
After breakfast and an optional morning walk, your professional driver-guide will pick you up from your camp and head out for an early morning game drive in the park. Explore the swampy areas where elephants and hippos are often seen bathing, and keep an eye out for a variety of plains game, antelopes, and numerous bird species. Enjoy stunning views of the park’s wildlife with Africa’s tallest free-standing mountain as a breathtaking backdrop—weather permitting. Stop mid-day for a picnic lunch before heading out for an afternoon game drive, following the tracks of different animals. In the evening, return to your camp for dinner and overnight stay.
* Main Destination: Amboseli National Park
* Meal Plan: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
* Accommodation: Ositua Rafiki Camp 
DAY 3: AMBOSELI-NAIROBI
Early in the morning, you'll enjoy your African breakfast, check out with packed picnic lunches for a Morning game drive in the park as you traverse from Amboseli Kimana Gate to Amboseli Mashenani Gate with at least 2 hours of game drive in search of more wildlife species and birds. You'll exit Amboseli National Park and drive back to Nairobi. Arriving in Nairobi late in the afternoon, you will be dropped at your hotel or at the airport to catch your flight.
* Main Destination: Nairobi
* Meal Plan: Breakfast & Lunch
* No accommodation
