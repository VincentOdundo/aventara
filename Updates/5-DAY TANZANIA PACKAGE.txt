DAY 1: ARUSHA – TARANGIRE NATIONAL PARK
After breakfast, you will be met in Arusha and depart for Tarangire National Park with a picnic lunch. Upon arrival, enjoy a full-day game drive in this beautiful park, famous for its large elephant herds, ancient baobab trees, and diverse wildlife including lions, giraffes, zebras, and antelopes. The Tarangire River attracts many animals, especially during the dry season. In the late afternoon, you will leave the park and drive to your tented camp for dinner and overnight.

Accommodation: Tarangire Campsite 
DAY 2: TARANGIRE – SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK
After breakfast, depart Tarangire and drive towards the legendary Serengeti National Park, passing through the scenic Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Enjoy beautiful landscapes along the way and stop for a picnic lunch. Upon entering Serengeti, you will begin a game drive across the vast plains, home to an incredible concentration of wildlife. You may see lions, cheetahs, elephants, and large herds of wildebeest and zebras. Continue with afternoon game viewing before heading to your camp for dinner and overnight.

Accommodation: Nyani Camp
DAY 3: FULL DAY IN SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK
Enjoy a full day of game drives in Serengeti National Park. After breakfast, set out with your guide to explore different areas of the park, depending on wildlife movements. The Serengeti is famous for the Great Migration and its high density of predators. You will have opportunities to see lions, leopards, cheetahs, hyenas, and many bird species. A picnic lunch will be served in the bush. After an exciting day of wildlife viewing, return to your camp for dinner and overnight.

Accommodation: Nyani Camp


DAY 4: SERENGETI – NGORONGORO CONSERVATION AREA
After breakfast, enjoy a final morning game drive in Serengeti as you exit the park. You will then drive towards the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, enjoying stunning views of the highlands. Lunch will be served en route. Upon arrival, you will ascend to the crater rim, where you can admire breathtaking views of the Ngorongoro Crater. Spend the evening at your lodge near the crater, relaxing and preparing for the next day’s adventure. Dinner and overnight at the camp.

Accommodation: Simba Campsite
DAY 5: NGORONGORO – LAKE MANYARA – ARUSHA
After an early breakfast, descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a game drive. This natural wonder is home to a high concentration of wildlife, including rhinos, lions, buffaloes, and flamingos. After the game drive and lunch, proceed to Lake Manyara National Park. Enjoy a short game drive in this scenic park, known for its tree-climbing lions and rich birdlife. In the afternoon, drive back to Arusha, marking the end of your safari.

Accommodation: Charity Hotel
